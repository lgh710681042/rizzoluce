Design

From its very beginning Rizzoluce® has been driven by design and technology. Glancing at the product range, one can immediately tell that innovation is the central impulse of development. Based on a passion for lighting and design, extensive trend and market research, and in-depth knowledge of product development, Rizzoluce’s Italian based design team over the years succeeded in creating a range of timeless, elegant and often industry changing lighting fixtures.
